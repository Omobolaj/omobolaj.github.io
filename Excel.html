Retail Customer Dashboard – Comprehensive Sales & Customer Insights

I realized the client was missing a unified view of their sales and customer data, which fragmented insights and slowed decisions. I took the initiative to build a comprehensive dashboard that integrates key sales figures, customer segments, and product performance into a single, interactive report.

By providing clear visualizations of revenue breakdowns, customer behaviors, and transaction trends, I empowered the leadership to quickly identify growth opportunities and customer preferences. This directly improved their ability to tailor marketing and sales strategies for maximum impact.

  • Tools Used: Microsoft Excel, advanced charts, PivotTables, slicers, dynamic formulas
  • Problem Solved: Fragmented data sources limited understanding of customer and sales dynamics
  • Outcome: Delivered actionable insights driving targeted sales efforts and boosting revenue

Profitability & Product Performance Insights

Tools Used: Microsoft Excel, PivotTables, Charts, Slicers, Conditional Formatting, Advanced Formulas

Problem Solved: I noticed the company struggled to get a clear picture of profits and product performance, which impacted growth planning and resource allocation.

Solution Provided: I designed a detailed dashboard capturing total revenue, costs, profits, and product-level trends, making complex data easily understandable for management.

Outcome: This enabled smarter decision-making, optimized resource use, and helped increase profitability by focusing on high-performing products and seasons.

Sales Performance & Market Insights

Tools Used: Microsoft Excel, Tables, Conditional Formatting, Formulas (SUMIF, IF statements), Data Validation, Charts

Problem Solved: The client was unable to monitor detailed monthly sales trends and product performance effectively, leading to missed sales and inventory opportunities.

Solution Provided: I built an interactive sales dashboard that tracks monthly sales dynamics, highlights top-selling products, and visualizes regional revenue streams to inform targeted actions.

Outcome: My solution provided clear visibility that improved stock planning, marketing focus, and ultimately boosted sales consistency and revenue growth.
