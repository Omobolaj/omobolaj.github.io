Retail Sales Dashboard – Comprehensive Performance Overview

I built this interactive Power BI dashboard to unify key retail KPIs revenue, sales volume, returns, product performance, and customer behavior. The challenge was to provide a 360° view of retail operations in one report.

This visual report helps leadership analyze performance by product, country, segment, and payment method. It tracks seasonal sales, identifies top-performing categories, and flags refund hotspots all in real time.

  • Tools Used: Power BI, DAX, slicers, matrix visual, stacked bar, line chart, drill-through filters
  • Problem Solved: Retail data was too distributed and disjointed for stakeholders to analyze quickly
  • Outcome: I implemented focused product adjustments and streamlined reporting workflows, which led to faster strategic pivots and a measurable boost in product sales performance.

Gadget Sales Intelligence Dashboard – Regional, Rep & Payment Insights

Tools Used: Power BI Desktop, DAX Measures, Card Visuals, Clustered Column Chart, Pie Chart, Line Chart, Slicers

Problem Solved: The sales team lacked centralized visibility into gadget performance across reps, regions, and payment methods. This slowed business decisions and limited the ability to respond to sales bottlenecks.

Solution Provided: I built an all-in-one Power BI dashboard that visualizes total sales, profit margins, rep performance, regional breakdowns, and payment preferences. Filters by year, month, and product line enable real-time exploration.

Outcome: With clear insights into monthly drops, underperforming regions, and rep contribution, the team reallocated sales targets, incentivized top performers, and optimized payment strategies. This led to smarter forecasting and higher monthly consistency in order fulfillment.

Pharmaceutical Sales Dashboard – Product Performance & Regional Profitability

Tools Used: Power BI Desktop, DAX, Power Query, Pie & Bar Visuals, Dynamic Filters

Problem Solved: The pharmaceutical company lacked a centralized view of product sales, profit margins, and regional performance, limiting their ability to make informed decisions on product distribution and customer focus.

Solution Provided: I designed an integrated dashboard that analyzes revenue and profit by drug name, customer accounts, and geographic zones. It includes profit margin analysis, tax summaries, and performance trends with filtering by year and product category.

Outcome: The commercial team used these insights to reallocate resources to top-performing drugs and regions, reduce overhead on low-profit segments, and maximize operational profitability.
